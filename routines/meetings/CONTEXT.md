# Meeting Prep & Follow-Up

**Type:** Routine
**Schedule:** Triggered 30 min before each meeting, and 1 hour after
**Last Run:** 2026-04-07

## Purpose

Automated routine that prepares context briefs before meetings and captures action items afterward. Ensures every meeting has a clear purpose going in and clear outcomes coming out.

## Pre-Meeting Workflow (T-30 min)

1. **Pull calendar event** — Get attendees, agenda, attached docs
2. **Gather context** — Search chat, inbox, and the project tracker for recent activity related to the meeting topic
3. **Build brief** — One-pager with: meeting goal, attendee roles, open questions, relevant updates since last meeting
4. **Surface prep items** — Flag any pre-reads or decisions needed

## Post-Meeting Workflow (T+60 min)

1. **Prompt for notes** — Ask for meeting notes or recording transcript
2. **Extract action items** — Parse notes for owners, deadlines, and deliverables
3. **Create to-do items** — Add extracted items to the to-do system with proper context
4. **Draft follow-up** — Prepare a follow-up message summarizing decisions and next steps
5. **Update workstreams** — If the meeting relates to an active workstream, update its CONTEXT.md

## Meeting Types Supported

| Type | Prep Depth | Follow-Up |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| 1:1 with manager | Light — recent updates, talking points | Action items only |
| Cross-functional sync | Medium — project status, blockers | Summary + action items |
| Leadership review | Heavy — data pulls, deck prep | Decisions + next steps email |
| External partner call | Heavy — partner context, deal status | Follow-up message draft |
| Sprint planning | Medium — backlog review, capacity | Sprint commitments |
