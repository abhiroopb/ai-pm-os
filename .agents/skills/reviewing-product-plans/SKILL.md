# Reviewing Product Plans

Use this skill to tighten a product plan before work begins.

## Review Order

1. Clarify the user problem and desired outcome.
2. Identify missing assumptions or evidence.
3. Check whether scope matches the stated goal.
4. Surface dependency, rollout, and stakeholder risks.
5. Check whether success metrics are clear enough to evaluate the work later.

## Default Output Shape

Keep the review practical:

- findings first
- open questions second
- recommended changes last

## What Good Looks Like

A strong review should answer:

- Is the problem real and specific?
- Is the proposed scope the smallest sensible version?
- What dependencies could stall the work?
- What should be measured before and after launch?
- What decisions are still vague enough to cause churn later?

## Helpful Inputs

When available, read:

- the draft plan or PRD
- the matching workstream `CONTEXT.md`
- related research or meeting notes
- any existing launch checklist or roadmap notes
