## Objective

Define Q3 2026 product roadmap priorities through a structured planning process. Balance investment across four pillars: growth (new users), activation (time-to-value), retention (reducing churn), and platform (technical foundation). Total engineering capacity: ~42 engineer-weeks.

## Planning Framework

Using a weighted scoring model across five dimensions:

| Dimension | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Revenue Impact | 30% | Estimated ARR impact in 12 months |
| User Impact | 25% | Number of users affected × severity |
| Strategic Alignment | 20% | Fit with 2026 company OKRs |
| Confidence | 15% | Data quality and estimation confidence |
| Effort Efficiency | 10% | Impact per engineering week |
